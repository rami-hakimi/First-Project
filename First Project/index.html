Yemen

Yemen, country situated at the southwestern corner of the Arabian Peninsula.It is mostly mountainous and generally arid, though there are broad patches with sufficient precipitation to make agriculture successful.The people speak various dialects of Arabic and are mostly Muslims.

Cities

Sanaa
Sanaa is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, although an exact date for its establishment is unknown. According to Yemeni legend, it was founded by Shem, one of the three sons of Noah.
Taiz
The historic city of Taiz is the third largest urban center in Yemen. A fortified settlement during the Sulayhid dynasty’s rule in the eleventh century, it later became an Ayyubid stronghold and the site of the Ayyubid treasury.
Aden
Adan, city of Yemen. It is situated along the north coast of the Gulf of Aden and lies on a peninsula enclosing the eastern side of Al-Tawāhī Harbour. The peninsula enclosing the western side of the harbour is called Little Aden.
